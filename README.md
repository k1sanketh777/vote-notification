## 📸 About the Educational Media Unit

The Educational Media Unit of Maris Stella College, Negombo is a dedicated platform that brings together creativity, innovation, and communication within the school community.

We provide students with opportunities to explore and develop skills in:

- 📷 **Photography & Videography** — Capturing school moments with creative visuals and storytelling
- 🎥 **Live Production** — Live streaming and production support for events and ceremonies
- 🎙️ **Announcing** — Professional announcing service for school events and ceremonies
- 🔧 **Technical** — Audio visual setup and technical assistance for school activities

Through active involvement in covering school events and creating visual content, the unit plays an essential role in documenting and presenting the vibrant life of the college.
