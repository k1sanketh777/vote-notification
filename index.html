ABOUT US

The Educational Media Unit of Maris Stella College is proudly recognized as one of the finest school media units in Negombo, setting the standard for creativity, innovation, and professionalism. Through exceptional photography, videography, graphic design, and live media coverage, we capture and showcase the true spirit of our college while inspiring excellence, teamwork, and passion in the next generation of media creators.
